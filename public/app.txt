Here's a suggested point system for the below password criteria:

1 Length: Award points based on the length of the password.
Example: 0.5 point for every  character included

2 Uppercase Letters: Award points if at least one uppercase letter is included.
Example: 2 points

3 Lowercase Letters: Award points if at least one lowercase letter is included.
Example: 2 points

4 Numbers: Award points if at least one number is included.
Example: 2 points

5 Symbols: Award points if at least one symbol is included.
Example: 2 points

6 No Character Repetition: Award points if there are no repeated characters.
Example: 3 points

7 Character Mix: Award points based on the diversity and balance of character types.
Example: 4 points

The maximum Point value, considering a maximum password length of 20 characters, would be 25.
The minimum Point value, considering a maximum password length of 20 characters, would be 6.
